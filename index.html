About Us

Anusaya Trading and IT Solutions is a Maharashtra-based organization focused on IT training, software services, and financial empowerment. We are committed to creating employment opportunities, building digital literacy, and promoting entrepreneurship through high-quality, government-certified training and consulting.

Our mission is to empower individuals with skills that match the current market demand in areas like IT, cybersecurity, and finance. With our ISO 9001:2015 certification, we aim to deliver excellence in service and education across rural and urban regions of Maharashtra.
